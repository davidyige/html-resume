David Y. Ige

8th Governor of Hawaii

December 1, 2014 - December 5, 2022

Highlights

Governor David Y. Ige was sworn in as the eighth governor of the State of Hawai‘i on December 1, 2014 and served two terms to December 5, 2022 (term limited). Governor Ige focused on improving the lives of Hawaiʻi’s people and making the islands a place future generations choose to call home. He increased affordable housing, reduced homelessness, accelerated moving toward the state’s 100% renewable energy goal, and remodeling public education to prepare students for the innovation economy of the 21st century. Under his leadership, the state has aggressively moved to ensure financial sustainability and enable future growth. He believes that we can achieve our shared goals because we have always been better together than alone.
